Typography

Fonts/typography, when used consistently, unifies messaging and creates familiarity. The following guidelines will help ensure maximum legibility and reinforcement of the SJI brand.

Benton Sans

Benton Sans is our primary typeface. Title and sentence case are preferred. Only use all caps in small instances such as client name. The default weight to use is Book | 500.

Headlines should be bold, all lowercaps, solid black #000.

Secondary Headline

Secondary headlines use two colors. The last 2 or three words in the statement are done in the two or three Brand Primary.

Example

we transform research and strategic insights into transformative creative.

Paragraphs

The default body text is 18px and color is #333. For optimum reading length, the paragraph width is capped at 70 characters, roughly 850px.

Example

We began by exploring different ways the Pinna brand could be expressed, before landing on a hand-drawn logo that perfectly represented the playful nature of the brand. Components

The SJI Website is made of repeatable modules that assemble into a consistant and orderly web experience.

Work Tile

The Work Tile is used throughout the site as a pathway component to lead to portfolio [work] pages. Tiles consist of these main elements:

  • Client Name: Always in Uppercase
  • Project Title: No more than 8 words.
  • Thumbnail Image of Project: Zoomed in and off-focus
  • Link to porfolio-single page

Hover State

The hover state should take 400ms to transition. The background is a Brand Primary Orange, with 16 pixels of padding around all 4 sides. The Client name, and Work name are displayed dead center in the box.

Home Carousel

The Home Carousel features the the 4 most prestigious works that SJI has worked on in the last year.

Each slide in the carousel has the following:

  • Title: The title will be pulled form the post and should not include the client name.
  • Image: 1500 x 700 Image for the background. The image is pulled in through CSS and centered vertically and horizontally within the slide space. Because of this, areas of the image will no always be visible on narrow monitors.
  • Cateogories:List of 2 - 4 Categories in all caps. This information is pulled form The Excerpt box on the portfolio item.
